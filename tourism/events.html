The Andean Calendar

El Terreno structures its seasons around the four Raymis — the great Andean ceremonies that mark the solstices and equinoxes. Each Raymi is an opportunity to experience living indigenous culture, to participate in ceremony, and to understand the cosmological framework that has sustained Andean communities for millennia.

  • Pawkar Raymi — February/March · Festival of flowering and abundance
  • Inti Raymi — June · Festival of the Sun · Summer solstice
  • Killa Raymi — September · Festival of the Moon · Equinox
  • Kapak Raymi — December · Festival of the Great Leader · Winter solstice

Carnaval de Guaranda

The most famous Carnaval in Ecuador takes place in Guaranda each February — a week of music, dance, colour and community celebration that draws visitors from across the country. El Terreno participants are welcome to join the festivities alongside the local community.
