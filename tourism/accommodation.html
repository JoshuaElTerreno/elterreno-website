Accommodation & Our Space

Set in the indigenous community of Atandahua, Guaranda — our land and buildings are home to volunteers, interns, associates and tourists alike.

The land

Our property sits in the community of Atandahua, on the outskirts of Guaranda, in the Bolívar province of the Ecuadorian Andes. The land is owned by the Cultural Exchange Centre and forms the physical foundation for all of our programmes and Foundation activities.

Accommodation

Participants stay in private rooms with local host families in close proximity to other international volunteers and interns. WiFi is available.

[ Photos of accommodation ]

Working spaces

We have dedicated working spaces for project development, mentoring sessions, workshops and community meetings.

[ Photos of working spaces ]

Community facilities

Shared cooking and dining areas, communal outdoor spaces, and access to the surrounding community landscape — market, city of Guaranda, and the wider Bolívar region.

Location
