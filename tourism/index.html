The region

Bolívar province sits in the central Andes of Ecuador, surrounded by some of the country's most dramatic landscapes. Within a few hours you can travel from high altitude páramo to tropical cloud forest. The region is home to Chimborazo — the world's highest volcano from the centre of the earth — and dozens of indigenous communities with living cultural traditions.
