Associates

Work alongside the founder and key stakeholders to design and implement strategies that will have long-term success. A minimum 12-week commitment for experienced professionals.

What's included

  • Accommodation with WiFi
  • Cooked lunches Monday–Friday
  • Site visits and field trips
  • Mentoring and coordination support
  • Certificate of completion

The role

As an Associate, you work 25–35 hours per week on El Terreno activities, choosing between a smaller time commitment with a contribution or longer hours at no cost.

What you'll do

  • Lead or support a strategic project
  • Collaborate with the founder and community stakeholders
  • Apply your professional skills in a real-world development context
  • Contribute to long-term organisational strategy
