About Us

01 — How We Got Here

Our Story

El Terreno was founded by Joshua Holmes in 2022. What began as a vision for a genuinely reciprocal cultural exchange programme has grown over five years into a multi-organisational platform for community development, sustainable tourism and international cooperation.

Joshua arrived in Guaranda in his mid-thirties, navigating his own life transition, and found something unexpected: a place where the depth of community and the richness of indigenous culture offered an antidote to everything that modern Western life seemed to be getting wrong.

Since 2022 we have delivered over 130 community workshops and events, welcomed participants from across the world, and built partnerships with government bodies, universities and international organisations. We are still small. Still building. And committed to doing this slowly, carefully and well — in the spirit of Ayni.

03 — How We Think

Our Philosophy

The Problem

In the race for material prosperity, the Global North has unintentionally created a breadth of crises — from climate to mental health, from meaning to AI — making our future very uncertain. We have lost our connection to community, nature, service and spirit: the things that bring true joy while enriching the world around us.

Indigenous peoples have existed for thousands of years in harmony with Mother Earth. Yet after centuries of oppression and globalisation that glamorises a Western lifestyle, many are disowning their cultures to migrate in search of a better life.

Each culture is facing a crisis. Each culture possesses what the other needs. But we only have a generation to prevent us losing everything.

The Solution

In the same way a diverse ecosystem is more abundant and resilient, so too are we as humanity when we experience meaningful cultural diversity. Through the respectful exchange of cultures, traditions and worldviews, we can create meaningful solutions to the greatest challenges of our time.

We are building a real-world microcosm of a more vibrant, prosperous and regenerative world.

We invite you to become a part of this mission in the heart of the Ecuadorian Andes.
