What the Foundation does

The Bolívar province is one of Ecuador's poorest regions. We work with communities to address the root causes of poverty, migration, and social inequality through 18 community project areas.

The challenge

40% of rural people in Bolívar live in poverty. Poor agricultural returns force migration to cities. 48% of children suffer malnutrition. 95% of surrounding communities are indigenous, with their culture under threat.

Our approach

We work with international volunteers, interns, and partners to deliver community-led projects across health, ecology, education, water, culture, and economic development. All work is done in collaboration with and at the direction of local communities.
