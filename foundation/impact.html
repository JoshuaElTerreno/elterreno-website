The context

40% of the rural population surrounding Guaranda lives in poverty. Due to lack of employment, education, infrastructure and healthcare, the area has high rates of child malnutrition (48%), teenage pregnancy (26%) and illiteracy (10%).

90% of rural communities are indigenous, but their culture and language are under threat as young people abandon their roots and migrate to cities in search of a better life.
